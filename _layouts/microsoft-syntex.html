Microsoft Syntex

Microsoft Syntex automates content capture, organization, and classification using AI and ML. It extracts valuable information from unstructured content and enhances search experiences. Improve content management and compliance with Microsoft Syntex. Unleash the potential of your unstructured data.

Intelligent Document Processing:

Syntex can automatically extract relevant information from documents, such as contracts, invoices, or forms. It can identify key data points,validate their accuracy, and populate structured databases or systems, reducing manual data entry and improving efficiency.
