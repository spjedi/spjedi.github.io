Power Platform

Power Platform is a suite of Microsoft technologies that enables custom business solutions. It includes Power Apps, Power Automate, Power BI, and Power Virtual Agents. With low-code/no-code tools, it supports app development, process automation, data analysis, and chatbot creation. Streamline processes, make data-driven decisions, and enhance experiences with Power Platform. Unleash your innovation today.

The Power Platform has been used to develop facility maintenance and inspection solutions, allowing organizations to streamline their maintenance processes and track inspection activities. An example is how the University of Michigan utilized Power Apps and Power Automate to create a facility inspection app. This app replaced paper-based inspections, improving efficiency, and enabling real-time reporting.
