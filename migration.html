Understanding Office 365 License Types

E1 License: Provides a very limited version of SharePoint to the company without the Office Suite
E3 License: Provides a full use SharePoint along with Office Suite
E4 License: Same as the E3, by Lync can make international calls
BI License: BI gives you tools that have to be driven off of perfect data. I currently sell a product that is more robust and a one time cost of $400 installed

On-Premise Types of Installation

Single Server: Set for a small company with very little documents
Two Tier Server: SharePoint hosted on one server and SQL on another. Used for small company with limited use
Three Tier Server: The most common installation due to be easy expansion as a company grows
Three Tier Server w/ Dedicated Search: Large amount of records with high use on search
Hybrid Model: Ability to sync with Office 365 or Azure for expanded use with On-Premise Tools
